Smoked Meat Restaurant

MEAT MOOT

Area
800 Sqm
Location
Al Khobar, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Meat Moot is a global smoked-meat restaurant shaped around a bold dining experience. The interior combines dark brick columns, raw concrete walls, exposed black ceilings, warm timber, stone counters, and burnt-orange leather seating. A central olive tree, open service counter, signage, and layered amber lighting soften the industrial setting, creating a lively, welcoming atmosphere that reflects the brand’s slow-smoked meat concept.
