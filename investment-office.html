Corporate Investment Office

INVESTMENT OFFICE

Area
380 Sqm
Location
Seef, Kingdom of Bahrain

This corporate office is designed around a refined and comfortable working environment. The interior combines soft white finishes, warm grey surfaces, natural timber, marble details, black metal framing, textured fabrics, and clear glass partitions. Integrated shelving, indoor planting, layered lighting, and relaxed lounge areas soften the workspace, creating a calm, professional atmosphere that feels modern, welcoming, and practical.
