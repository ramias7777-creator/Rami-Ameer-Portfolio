Modern Steakhouse Project

Swiss Butter

Swiss Butter is a casual steakhouse centered around a simple, signature dining experience. The space carries an industrial yet warm character, combining exposed brick, raw concrete, black ceiling details, metal chairs, timber tabletops, and bold green feature elements. Hanging greenery, open counter views, and warm ambient lighting soften the interior, creating a cozy nighttime atmosphere that feels casual, energetic, and memorable.

Miette Cafe is a French bakery and cafe designed around a warm and welcoming cafe experience. 04

Smoked Meat Restaurant

Meat Moot

Meat Moot is a global smoked-meat restaurant shaped around a bold dining experience. The interior combines dark brick columns, raw concrete walls, exposed black ceilings, warm timber, stone counters, and burnt-orange leather seating. A central olive tree, open service counter, signage, and layered amber lighting soften the industrial setting, creating a lively, welcoming atmosphere that reflects the brand’s slow-smoked meat concept.
