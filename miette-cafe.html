French Bakery & Cafe

MIETTE CAFE

Area
180 Sqm
Location
Seef, Kingdom of Bahrain

Miette Cafe is a French bakery and cafe designed around a warm and welcoming cafe experience. The interior combines soft cream finishes, fluted textures, light oak, olive-green accents, woven seating, and subtle bronze details. Greenery, freshly baked goods, and warm lighting create a cozy and relaxing atmosphere, offering a pleasant setting to enjoy coffee, fresh bread, pastries, and French delights.
